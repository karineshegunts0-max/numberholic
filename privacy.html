Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2026

Karine Shegunts ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Numberholic mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use our App.

Information We Collect

Numberholic does not require you to create an account and does not ask you for personally identifiable information such as your name or email address. However, the App uses third-party services that may collect information, primarily for advertising purposes.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

We use Google AdMob to display ads in the App. AdMob may collect and use data such as:

  • Device identifiers (e.g., advertising ID / IDFA)
  • Device information (model, operating system version)
  • General location (derived from IP address)
  • Ad interaction data (ad views, clicks)

This data is used to serve relevant ads and measure ad performance. Learn more about how Google uses data from apps that use its services: policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites. To learn about AdMob's data practices, see Google's Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy.

Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores your game progress locally on your device, including high scores, coins, daily streaks, and best streaks. This data stays on your device and is not transmitted to us.

Children's Privacy

Numberholic is suitable for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so we can remove it.

App Tracking Transparency (iOS)

On iOS, the App may ask for your permission to track your activity for advertising purposes, in accordance with Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework. You can change this permission at any time in Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

Your Choices

  • You can reset or limit ad tracking in your device settings (Settings → Privacy & Security → Apple Advertising / Tracking).
  • You can delete the App at any time, which removes all locally stored game data.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes are effective when posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
