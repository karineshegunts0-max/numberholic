About the game

Numberholic is a fast-paced math arcade game that turns mental math into pure fun. Numbers fall from the sky — solve each equation before it reaches the bottom. Build combos, beat your high score, and climb through 6 unique worlds.

  • 6 colorful worlds, from simple addition to powers & percentages
  • Combo multipliers and satisfying score popups
  • Daily bonus rewards and high-score tracking
  • Quick sessions — a perfect brain workout
